Who Are We

Quality

Why Titan Pipes?

  • Made from 100% virgin PE materials
  • Non-toxic and odor-free
  • UV and chemical resistance
  • Flexible and abrasion resistant
  • High flow with minimal friction loss

Quality Assurance

  • Dimensional accuracy checks
  • Creep rupture and hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Carbon black dispersion tests
  • Tensile strength & elongation analysis

Crush Resistance

Our pipes offer excellent crush resistance and microbial protection, especially important for underground applications.

Handling & Storage

  • Use PPE and appropriate lifting tools
  • Do not drag or drop pipes
  • Store in a dry, shaded, and level area

Certifications

We comply with BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) and other industry regulations to ensure our HDPE and PVC pipes meet the highest quality benchmarks.
